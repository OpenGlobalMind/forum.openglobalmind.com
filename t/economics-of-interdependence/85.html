The meltdown of 2008 was largely a result of the financialization of real estate assets; turning them into phantom financial vehicles with a calculated market value far above their real value; thus it created a bubble. Financialization leads to specious values that are unsustainable. Keeping the assets local, like letting people sell houses to support local business expansion with the revenue stream like we are doing with starfinancialsolutions.co is one way to keep the assets local even though mortgages can still be used to make more profitable, short term loans available to fuel community economic development.
